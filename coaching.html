LET'S TALK
For Owners, Executives & Sales Teams

Coaching for people with a number to hit.

Whether you own the business or run a piece of one, the job is the same: results, through people, under pressure. That's the world Joe spent thirty years in. Here's how the work works.

Thirty years in enterprise sales at Cisco · Veeam · Protegrity · Red Lion

Coaching or consulting. Know the difference.

Coaching is ongoing. We meet on a regular cadence, set real goals, and I hold you accountable to them. It's for leaders who want to grow and want a partner who won't let them off the hook.

Consulting is tactical. You have a specific problem: a sales process that leaks, a team that's stalled, a plan that needs an outside eye. We scope it, solve it, done.

Most clients start with one and end up using both. We'll figure out which fits in the first conversation.

Who this is for

Business owners.

You're past startup and stuck at a plateau. Revenue is fine but flat. You're working more hours than year one and can't see why. Coaching gives you the structure to work on the business instead of just in it: pricing, delegation, pipeline, the decisions you've been putting off.

Executives.

New role, bigger team, higher stakes. Or same role, and the ceiling is starting to feel real. Executive coaching sharpens the skills that got you here and builds the ones that get you further: communication, strategic thinking, and leading people who don't think like you.

Sales teams and financial advisors.

Group coaching for teams that need a common language and a process everyone runs. Joe has trained and led sales organizations at the enterprise level. He knows the difference between a motivational speech and a system, and he only sells the system.

How engagements run

One-on-one or group. Virtual or in person around North Jersey. Engagements are structured through the FocalPoint methodology, which means there's an actual curriculum underneath the conversations, not just vibes and a whiteboard.
